## The project

Remote fullstack internship with a team distributed between Chile and Puebla, Mexico. The goal was to land a working MVP for Exoap's web platform — a startup connecting Mexican SMBs with internationalization experts.

## My role

- Built UI components for the public site.
- Documented the project and helped distribute tasks across the team.
- Coordinated with product to scope the MVP.

## Takeaways

First time in a 100% remote, multicultural team. Learned how critical written documentation, tight standups, and ticket discipline are when you don't share an office with your teammates.
