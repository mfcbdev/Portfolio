## The context

The IT Agility team needed continuous visibility into squad health: how long a story takes from backlog entry to production, what percentage of changes cause failures, where flows stall.

## What I did

- Defined extraction pipelines from **Azure DevOps** using WIQL to pull work items, transforming them with scripts to feed **Looker Studio**.
- Designed a set of reports with actionable DORA metrics: **Cycle Time, Lead Time, Change Failure Rate** per squad and per sprint.
- Supported the team in reading the reports during ceremonies, translating numbers into improvement hypotheses.

## What I took away

A good report isn't the one showing the most metrics — it's the one that triggers the next right conversation. Working inside a bank gave me rigor on documentation, access control, and handling sensitive data.
